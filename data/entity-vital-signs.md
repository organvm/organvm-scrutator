## METRIC DEFINITIONS

### Pathos (Emotional/Resonance Health)
- **Range:** 0.0 - 1.0
- **Measures:** Audience connection, emotional resonance, engagement potential
- **Vitals:**
  - Connection Score: How well does it resonate with humans?
  - Engagement Index: Will anyone care?
  - Narrative Strength: Does it tell a story worth following?

### Logos (Logical/Rational Health)
- **Range:** 0.0 - 1.0
- **Measures:** Internal consistency, evidence quality, rational appeal
- **Vitals:**
  - Coherence Score: Does it contradict itself?
  - Evidence Density: Facts per assertion
  - Argument Strength: Can it survive scrutiny?

### Ethos (Authority/Credibility Health)
- **Range:** 0.0 - 1.0
- **Measures:** Trustworthiness, expertise signals, reliability
- **Vitals:
  - Trust Index: Would you bet on this?
  - Authority Signals: Credentials, citations, references
  - Track Record: History of delivery vs. promises
